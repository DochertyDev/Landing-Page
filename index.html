About Me

I believe technology should empower, not complicate. My journey into Salesforce, AI solutions, and technology all started with curiosity and questions. "How does it work?", "What does it do?", and "Who does it benefit?". These are questions I have been asking myself nearly my entire life, constantly replacing the "it" with new variables.

Over the past three years of consulting, I've evolved from learning the ropes as an Associate to leading client engagements as a Solutions Consultant. My passion lies in bridging the gap between technical possibilities and business realities, translating ideas into working solutions that drive real value.

What drives me most is staying on top of the fast-moving tech landscape, researching emerging tools and frameworks, applying them, and simplifying the concepts for unfamiliar audiences. The moment a client sees their vision come to life in a demo, or when a custom dashboard reveals insights they never knew existed. I thrive on continuous learning, whether it's mastering the latest Salesforce AI capabilities like Agentforce, building privacy-focused tools like my personal RedactFlow project, or exploring cutting-edge technologies like Model Context Protocol.

When I'm not thinking about solutions or configuring Salesforce environments, you'll find me experimenting with new AI frameworks, working on open-source projects, or helping others understand how intelligent automation can transform their workflows.
